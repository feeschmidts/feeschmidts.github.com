Open Utility

Open Utility is an early stage startup, part of the Bethnal Green Incubator program Summer 2013 at Nesta. Open Utility aims to revolutionise the energy industry by allowing people with micro generations to easily sell their energy to fairer prices directly to other people. OpenUtility allows energy consumers to choose where to purchase their energy from, by buying it directly from individuals or local community projects.
