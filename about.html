Hello!

I like to design fun and meaningful new services and tools which allow people to collaborate, learn and create. My work is user centred with a focus on co-creation mainly in social or environmental projects. I would like to gain more experience designing web and native apps in a close knit team made up of developers and other designers which would ideally also provide an opportunity to deepen my frontend skills with the intent to be able to create interactive prototypes for testing and more insight into UI. I like to get stuck into projects and am trying to work transparently in an agile manner for better teamwork and progress.
