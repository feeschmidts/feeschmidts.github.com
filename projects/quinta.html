Quinta das Relvinhas

The Quinta is a guest house set amongst the rolling hills of the Serra de Algarve in walking distance from Monchique. The house is an old villa counting as one of the oldest and proudest of the area.

During summer the Quinta das Relvinhas offers a family all inclusive programm. During winter however the facilities are more or less vacant with the occasional walking group.

How can the Quinta promote itself to cater for greater varieties of activities?

Website: Before

From holiday house to activity space

New potential activities and target groups include group seminars and workhops (art, painting, writing, knitting, coaching), sport groups (walking, surfing, climbing, cycling), wellness groups (meditation, yoga, relaxation, 3 element cooking, raw foods, forging, wild mushrooms), event bookings (wedding, teamwork, hackathons, seminars) and individual bookings (backpackers, hostel, lodging).

Versatile Identity & touchpoints

A versatile identity and communication promoting what the house can be booked for. This website communication needs to be backed up by flyers and meeting potential groups.
