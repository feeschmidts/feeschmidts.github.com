Commonplace

I'm looking after everything design and usability related at Commonplace. This includes the design and QA of all features, branding, production of marketing materials and supporting customers on their Commonplaces.

Achievements

  • Iterating and evolving the Commonplace platform and its tools

    In the three years I've been with Commonplace we've grown to 80+ customers, 180 Commonplaces internationally, 30K users, 55K comments and 45K agreements.

  • AA accessibility accreditation

    In less than three months we reworked our tools and pages to become AA accessibility accredited.

  • Award for best stakeholder engagement 2017

    2017 planning awards for the Commonplace Mini-holland programme with Waltham Forest.

  • Supporting and advising customers on their digital public engagement

    Customers including Waltham Forest on the Mini-holland programme, Newcastle City Council, Bristol City Council, Sustrans, HS2, NHS North West London, Grainger, Grosvenor and TRL for the GATEway autonomous car trials in Greenwich.
