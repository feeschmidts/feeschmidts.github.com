How to sell Nature to kids and parents?

Spotted! App

Spotted app is an outcome of Wildthing by GoodforNothing - a regular Design-led multidisciplinary hackathon event. Wildthing collaborated with David Nash and Green Lions to make Nature tasty to kids and parents encouarging kids to go out of the house and learn discover stuff. Ideas developed during Wildthing is taken forward and prototyped with kids.

The ‘Walkways of Monchique’ are packed with geourgeous nature, classified species and funny creatures. These five new maps package activities as themed maps, which tourists can download and print themselves or get given by guest houses included in the information pack when renting.

Presentation

Veredas de Monchique website had 309 views in the first two weeks of existance despite Christmas and December being a low season month. Since it has supplied maps to 1,780 visitors from 26 different countries with the majority visiting the site from Portugal.

My Role

As the initiator of the idea and concept, I worked with team members and local businesses to collect the content for the maps. I prototyped and GSP traced routes and researched existing walks in Monchique and the Algarve on how the mapped trails differentiate themselves in terms of themes and information on mushrooms, flora and fauna. I worked with two local organisations together to create 2 new walk ways (Tree Walk and Caldas Map). I also prototyped numerous maps and worked with Janki, our illustrator, on the final ones.

Post project I am maintaining the site, responding to comments and got in contact with Monchique villa rental businesses and Monchique blogs for linking into improving Google searchability and raising awareness.
