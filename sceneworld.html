Making a Commodore64 Magazine fit for the here and now.

Scene World

Scene World is a disk mag magazine for the Commodore 64. Every three months since 2001 an international team publishes a new Scene World magazine with interviews and reflections of people who worked on applications, games, music and graphics for the first personal computer.

I redesigned the Scene World website to allow traction and access to a bigger community, using current web frameworks, integrating social channels and videos. The diskmag itself is still published in C64 format, we normal people require an emulator to view its content.

Go to sceneworld.org
